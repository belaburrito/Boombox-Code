Collision was founded in 2005 by industry veterans with a passion for games. Bringing together experienced talent from the Games, Film, and Television industries, Collision has a unique view to both existing and original entertainment franchises.

Collision Studios released 300: March to Glory alongside the Frank Miller / Zack Snyder film 300, and has since become a Greatest Hits list title for the Sony PSP, selling hundreds of thousands of copies around the world.

Collision Studios is licensed to develop on all major console and handheld platforms, including the Sony PlayStation 2, PlayStation Portable, PlayStation 3, Nintendo DS, Wii, as well as the Microsoft Xbox 360.

Collision Studios employees hail from Activision, EA, Warner Brothers, THQ, Digital Eclipse, Pandemic, Insomniac, SCEA, Universal Studios, Rockstar Games, Psygnosis and more, with expeirence on all platforms and genres, with licensed and original IP.
